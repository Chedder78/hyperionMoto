About Velocity Moto

Redefining Custom Supersport Motorcycles

Founded in 2010, Velocity Moto has established itself as the premier builder of custom supersport motorcycles for discerning enthusiasts worldwide.

Our philosophy is simple: every motorcycle should be as unique as its rider. We reject mass production in favor of bespoke craftsmanship, where attention to detail and performance are paramount.

With a team of engineers, designers, and master fabricators, we push the boundaries of what's possible in motorcycle design and performance.

From our state-of-the-art facility in Motor Valley, California, we create approximately 50 custom motorcycles each year, each one a testament to our commitment to excellence.
